About ULIC

Pioneers of Innovation, Architects of the Future

Our Genesis

From a foundational idea to a force of change.

October 2024

The University Laboratory ICT Club (ULIC) was born from a vision to create a hub for tech enthusiasts. Founded as one of the nine founding clubs of University Laboratory School and College, we were driven by a passion for digital creation and problem-solving.

January 2025

Our commitment to collaboration and structure led us to a pivotal moment. ULIC took the initiative to establish the United Club Association (UCA), an organization designed to manage and unify all clubs within the institution, positioning ULIC as a leader in governance and innovation.

Feb — April 2025

A period of intense incubation and refinement. We dedicated these months to polishing our internal structures and experimenting with various prototypes. This phase was crucial for brainstorming ideas that would later define the betterment of both the club and the association.

May 2025

ULIC officially took on the ambitious project of developing a comprehensive Web App. This marked the transition from planning to execution, setting the stage for a digital transformation of club management.

July 2025

We extended our technical expertise to the wider community, providing critical website support to the Photography Club (ULPC) and Language Club (ULLC) for their landmark event, "Frame of Revolution: Bangladesh 2.0".

August 2025

A major milestone achieved: The official release of the ULIC Web App. This platform revolutionized how our members interact, manage projects, and access resources.

Sept — Oct 2025

Launch of the Season System. This innovative framework was introduced to encourage continuous learning, gamify participation, and foster a culture of sharing tech brilliance among members.

December 2025

Looking ahead to the mobile frontier, we initiated the project for the launch of our dedicated Mobile App(s), aiming to bring the ULIC experience to everyone's pocket.

Our Vision

We see a future where technology is a universal language for creation. Our dream is not confined to Dhaka or even Bangladesh; we believe the entire world is our stage, and we are preparing our members to shine on it.

Our Mission

To cultivate a dynamic environment where students can explore, learn, and master technology. We are dedicated to pushing boundaries, fostering innovation, and empowering the next generation of tech leaders and creators.

Pioneering Accomplishments

Setting records and redefining what a club can achieve.

Digital Infrastructure

ULIC proudly manages the entire online infrastructure for the UCA, from websites to internal systems, ensuring a seamless digital experience for all associated clubs.

Software Firsts

We hold the distinction of being the first club to develop and launch custom web applications, desktop software, and mobile apps, setting a new benchmark for technical achievement.

A Unique Identity

ULIC stands as one of the most unique ICT clubs in Bangladesh, recognized for our ambitious projects, technical prowess, and unwavering commitment to innovation.
